# ARS Governance

## Founding steward

ARS was conceived by Wesley Snow, Founder of RANKIGI, on 18 May 2026. RANKIGI is the founding reference implementation for Layer 2 (EPO).

The standard is open. RANKIGI does not own ARS. RANKIGI is one implementation of one layer.

## Principles

- The standard belongs to the ecosystem.
- Any layer can have multiple conformant implementations.
- No single vendor controls the spec.
- Decisions are made in the open.
- The founding steward commits to a patent non-assertion covenant: no patents held by RANKIGI Inc. will be asserted against any conformant implementation of ARS.

## Decision making

**Currently:** the founding steward reviews and merges proposals.

**Target:** move to a neutral foundation (Linux Foundation or OpenSSF) at v1.0 with a formal technical steering committee and a two-implementation rule for graduating versions.

## Contributing

Open an issue or pull request. All contributions are welcome. Spec changes require at least one independent review before merging. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Patent non-assertion

RANKIGI Inc. commits that no patents held by RANKIGI Inc. will be asserted against any party implementing the ARS specification in conformance with the published spec text.
